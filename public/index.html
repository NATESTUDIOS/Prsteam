About PRST

P.R.S.T is an abbreviation of Problem Solving Team - a community consisting of individuals with diverse skillsets who come together to put their skills to practice by working on projects that solve problems in the community around them.

The initiative was co-founded to address the reality that there are many skilled people waiting for their next gig instead of also putting their skills to good use in their communities.

We believe that by bringing together talented individuals from different backgrounds, we can create innovative solutions to local challenges while building meaningful connections and gaining valuable experience.

Our Mission & Values

Community Impact

We focus on identifying and solving real problems that affect our local communities, creating tangible positive change.

Collaboration

We bring together people with diverse skills and backgrounds to work on projects that no single person could accomplish alone.

Skill Application

We provide a platform for skilled individuals to apply their talents to meaningful projects beyond their regular work.
